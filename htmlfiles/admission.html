Admission Process

Step-by-Step Guide to Admissions

  • Online Application: Visit our website and fill out the online application form with accurate details.
  • Document Submission: Submit the required documents along with the application form.
  • Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview.
  • Admission Confirmation: Successful candidates will receive an admission confirmation letter.

Admission Requirements

Documents Required at the time of Admission

  • Proof Of Age: Original Birth Certificate (Issued by Municipal Corporation)
  • Academic Records: Mark sheets and certificates of previous academic years
  • Identity Proof: Aadhar Card, or any government-issued ID
  • Passport-Sized Photographs: Recent photographs of the student
  • Medical Certificate: A certificate from a registered medical practitioner confirming the student's health status
